A facilitated assume-breach exercise where your team investigates, decides, and acts against a real APT scenario mapped to your stack. Not a training course, but a live decision-making simulation that validates playbooks, exposes gaps, and builds incident response muscle memory for cybersecurity teams in Quebec and Canada.

Custom scenarios mapped to your sector: Finance, Legal, Healthcare, or Tech/SaaS. The attack chain is built from real threats targeting your industry.
Assume-breach with real APT TTPs: We select threat actors that actually target organizations like yours, then simulate their post-compromise behavior.
Mixed-team exercises: Technical and executive participants in the same room: IR, dev, legal, comms, and leadership all run the same scenario.
Real-time decision logging: Every decision is captured with timestamp, decision-maker, and rationale, the raw material for your playbook gaps report.
Playbook validation and gap analysis: We test your existing playbooks against the scenario. If no playbook exists, the report tells you exactly what to write first.
5 to 10 participants, 3 to 6 hours: Scoped to fit your team: half-day or full-day format, virtual or in-person.
// Deliverables
  • Executive summary: decisions made, gaps identified, risk posture assessment
  • Exercise timeline reconstruction
  • Lessons learned document
  • Playbook validation report: gaps, recommendations, and creation guidance if no playbook exists
  • Decision log: what was decided, by whom, and when
  • Optional follow-up: remediation roadmap session
