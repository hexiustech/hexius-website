OSINT & External Exposure Assessment: Open Source Intelligence, Attack Surface Mapping

Request a Quote See full details

We map exactly what attackers see before they target you: people, systems, and data. Findings are organized by exploitability and translated into real business risk with prioritized remediation steps.

People: employees, management, and C-suite: Emails, credentials, social footprint, C-Suite and their assistants contacts, and role exposure. Who can be targeted and exactly how an attacker would approach them.
Exposed systems: subdomains and uninventoried infrastructure: Forgotten staging environments, exposed tech stacks, and misconfigured cloud assets visible from the outside. Attack surface you may not know exists.
Data and metadata: documents, org structure, and leaked records: Public documents with embedded metadata, product and facility information, and credential leaks from past breaches indexed in public sources.
Business risk translation: not just an inventory: Every finding is mapped to an attacker use case: how it enables phishing, lateral movement, or access escalation, and what it costs your organization if exploited.
Dark web and breach data: Leaked credentials, internal data dumps, and dark web mentions correlated against your organization and key personnel.
Prioritized remediation by category: Findings organized by people, systems, and data, with specific remediation steps that reduce real-world exposure, not a generic checklist.

  • Subdomain and infrastructure inventory with risk assessment
